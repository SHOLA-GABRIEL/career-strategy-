06 Risk Assessment

POTENTIAL BLOCKERS

01
Weak Online Presence
Empty LinkedIn, no GitHub READMEs, no profile photo. Certs and projects become invisible without a storefront.
02
Can't Pitch Your Work
Can't explain your project in 60 seconds? Hiring managers move on. Practice your project pitch daily out loud.
03
Resume Has No Metrics
"I built X" loses to "Built X that reduced manual review time by 80%." Metrics signal impact, not just activity.
04
Wrong Role Targeting
Applying to senior roles or wrong stacks wastes time. 10 targeted applications beat 100 random ones every time.
05
Location Bias (Nigeria)
Beatable. Target UK, Canada, EU startups. Set up Payoneer/Wise. Pitch your WAT timezone overlap as an advantage.
06
Giving Up Before Week 10
Average job search = 40–60 apps before first response. Most people quit at week 3. Winners are still sending at week 10.
